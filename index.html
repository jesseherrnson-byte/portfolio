Jesse Herrnson is a software engineer, game designer, educator, musician, and lover of puzzles, film, TV, and any kind of interactive storytelling.

At a young age, Jesse was introduced to video games and technology, and he quickly became obsessed with learning how computers worked and how to make things for them. It was that passion for "making" that set Jesse on a journey to study software engineering and find his passion for creating interactive experiences and education.

Now, Jesse is game designer and full-stack engineer competent in multiple programming languages, AI, and the iterative design process.
