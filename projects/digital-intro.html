During my time at Wesleyan University, I worked for Professor Lisa Dierker in creating materials for a project called Digital Intro. Digital Intro is a novel approach to Wesleyan’s PSYC105 Foundations of Contemporary Psychology course which has students “explore concepts and content in the field of psychology through video storytelling, programming, data visualization, web development, design and more.”

As part of my work for this course, I created educational video content which students would watch throughout the course as supplementary material to readings. These videos were created using Adobe Premiere and Photoshop. For the sections I worked on, I took inspiration from many educational Youtube channels I watched as a kid, including CGP Grey and LEMMiNO. You can see the videos I worked on (Emotion and Language) on the course website on the "Video" tab.
